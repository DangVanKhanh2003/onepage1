Unlocking Sustainability:

BREEAM Certification

for Real Estate


A renowned global system for assessing the environmental impact of real estate properties. D-Group collaborates with a leading European company to offer consulting services for BREEAM certification, which:


  • Employs a research-based visual assessment system

  • Positively influences building design, construction, and operation

  • Establishes high-quality, stable technical standards

  • Encourages innovative solutions to reduce environmental impacts


This certification addresses sustainable development and environmental protection, allowing developers to showcase the benefits of eco-efficient technologies.
